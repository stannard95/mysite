Youtube

For the past two years I have managed my own YouTube channel.

I saw other people online making montages from their favourite TV shows. I wanted to do this for views, so once I saved up for a laptop that could handle the software i began.

At first I was...Bad. But like most things with practice I began to improve. Soon enough a few of my videos were watched over 50,000 times and even retweeted by some of the actors that played the character in the subject of my video. Currently I have a view total over 350,000 and growing everyday.
